Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich. A grilled cheese sandwich is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread, which was originally filled with American cheese, but can be made with any assortment of cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Texas toast bread or sourdough bread
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • Sliced medium cheddar, gouda, and Havarti cheese

Steps

  1. Spread half a tablespoon of butter on each side of slice bread
  2. Heat the skillet over low to medium/low heat. Add the two slices of bread butter side down
  3. Add cheese on the pieces of bread and cover the sandwich with the other piece of toast.
  4. Continue to brown the bread until both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  5. Cut the sanwich in half on a diagonal and serve.
